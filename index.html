Featured Projects

Selected examples of strategy translated into execution and measurable business outcomes.

New Business Line Creation

Challenge: 3.6M MT of low-grade nickel was treated as non-commercial waste.
Execution: Built the business case, led commissioning, established in-house operations, SOP, and cost controls.
Impact: Created a new IDR 50B+ CAPEX business line and reduced handling cost.

Organization Transformation

Challenge: Decision-making was centralized, slow, and dependent on individuals.
Execution: Designed structure, governance, SOP, roles, and accountability systems.
Impact: Reduced decision cycle from 5 days to 1 day and improved compliance across 12 business units.

Commercial Launch

Challenge: No trading business, team, or sourcing structure existed.
Execution: Built the trading model, supplier network, pricing flow, and operating structure.
Impact: Reached $2.8M monthly revenue and ~$200K monthly gross margin within 3 months.

Data Transformation

Challenge: Reporting was manual, slow, and lacked real-time visibility.
Execution: Built centralized databases, KPI frameworks, dashboards, and governance.
Impact: Reduced reporting time from 4 hours to 5 minutes and enabled real-time decisions.
