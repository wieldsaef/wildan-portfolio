Business Build

Turning Idle Assets into a Revenue-Generating Business

Built a new business line from non-commercial nickel inventory through strategic modeling, operational build-out, and cost restructuring.

What made this work was not only recognizing the opportunity inside idle material, but building the financial logic, operating model, and execution structure required to convert it into a commercially viable business.

3.6M MT idle material monetized IDR 50B+ CAPEX ~$1/ton cost reduction 1.5+ years stable operations
Problem Low-grade nickel was treated as waste with no commercial path and high contractor dependency.
Move Built full business case, commissioned operations, and shifted into a structured in-house model.
Impact Created a new business line from previously idle material with lower handling cost and sustained execution.

Business Challenge

The issue was not only underutilized inventory — it was the absence of a commercial and operating model that could justify monetization.

  • Low-grade nickel (<0.9) treated as non-commercial waste
  • ~3.6M MT stockpiled with zero economic value
  • High contractor dependency driving elevated handling cost
  • No internal capability to process or monetize the material

Strategic Execution

The solution required more than optimization. It required building a business from first principles — financially, operationally, and structurally.

  • Developed full business case and financial model covering CAPEX, OPEX, and payback
  • Led vendor sourcing, negotiation, and end-to-end commissioning in ~5 months
  • Built in-house operations and removed contractor dependency
  • Designed SOP, operating model, cost control system, and team structure

Business Impact

The outcome was not a process improvement — it was the creation of a new revenue-generating operating line.

  • Monetized ~3.6M MT previously idle material
  • Established a new business line with IDR 50B+ CAPEX investment
  • Reduced handling cost by approximately $1/ton
  • Sustained stable operations for 1.5+ years
