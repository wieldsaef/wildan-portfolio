  • ~3.6M MT stockpiled with zero economic value
  • High contractor dependency driving elevated handling cost
  • No internal capability to process or monetize material

Strategic Execution

  • Develop full business case and financial model (CAPEX, OPEX, payback)
  • Lead vendor sourcing, negotiation, and end-to-end project commissioning
  • Build in-house operations and eliminate contractor dependency
  • Design SOP, operating model, and cost control system
  • Build and lead team of 150+ personnel

Business Impact

  • Transform non-commercial waste into a revenue-generating asset
  • Monetize ~3.6M MT previously idle material
  • Reduce handling cost by ~$1/ton
  • Establish a new business line with IDR 50B+ CAPEX investment
  • Sustain stable operations for 1.5+ years
